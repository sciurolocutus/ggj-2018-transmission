You wake up and get in your car to go to work. Then you realize you're actually your car's transmission. But you're still running late for work.

Controls:

  • Up: shift up
  • Down: shift down
  • Right: attempt to speed up (but you don't control the gas pedal)
  • Left: attempt to slow down (but you don't control the brake pedal)
  • Shift: start the radio (because for some reason you control that)
  • Escape: Crash the game (and the car)
